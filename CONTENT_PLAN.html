02.4
Single Project Detail
Per-project page · Template
Needs Review
Page structure (template — same for every project)
Section 1 — Breadcrumb
Work / [Discipline] / [Project Name]
Section 2 — Project Hero
Full-width hero image. Eyebrow: "Discipline · Location". H1: Project name.
Section 3 — Spec Bar
4 facts: Industry · Area · Year · Scope (e.g. Turnkey). Terracotta top rule.
Section 4 — Project Body
Left: editorial lead (Cormorant Garamond italic). Right: 2–3 body paragraphs + materials tags.
Section 5 — Image Gallery
3-image grid: 1 large (left, full height) + 2 stacked (right). Photo captions optional.
Section 6 — Prev / Next
Links to adjacent projects. Auto-populated from the project list.
Example — Meridian Medical Pavilion (reference file)
"Calm by design: daylight, honed stone, and warm timber soften a clinical brief into something restorative."
